On those grounds, 
THE COURT 
hereby: 
1. Annuls Commission Regulation (EC) No 1107/96 of 12 June 1996 on the registration of geographical indications and designations of origin under the procedure laid down in Article 17 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 2081/92 to the extent to which it registered `Feta' as a protected designation of origin; 
2. Orders the Commission of the European Communities to pay the costs; 
3. Orders the Hellenic republic to bear its own costs.
