

1.
 Declares that, by laying down a general prohibition of health-related information on the labelling of foodstuffs for general
            consumption and by subjecting the display of such information to a prior authorisation procedure, the Republic of Austria
            has failed to fulfil its obligations under Articles 2(1)(b) and 15(1) and (2) of Council Directive 79/112/EEC of 18 December
            1978 on the approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to the labelling, presentation and advertising of foodstuffs,
            as amended by Directive 97/4/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 January 1997; 
            

2.
 Orders the Republic of Austria to pay the costs; 
            

3.
 Orders the Kingdom of Denmark to bear its own costs.
