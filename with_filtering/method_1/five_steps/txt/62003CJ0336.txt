On those grounds, the Court (First Chamber) hereby rules:
Article 3(2) of Directive 97/7/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 May 1997 on the protection of consumers
               in respect of distance contracts is to be interpreted as meaning that ‘contracts for the provision of transport services’
               includes contracts for the provision of car hire services.
Language of the case: English.
