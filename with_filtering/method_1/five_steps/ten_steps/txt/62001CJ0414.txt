On those grounds, 
THE COURT 
(Second Chamber) 
hereby: 
1. Declares that by failing to adopt within the prescribed period the laws, regulations and administrative provisions necessary to comply with Directive 97/7/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 May 1997 on the protection of consumers in respect of distance contracts the Kingdom of Spain has failed to fulfil its obligations under Article 15(1) of that directive; 
2. Orders the Kingdom of Spain to pay the costs.
